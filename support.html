WellTimed Support

WellTimed is a time tracking app for Apple Watch that helps you balance positive habits and negative time sinks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a timer?

Tap an activity from the home screen or the Activities tab. You can also long press an activity to start timing immediately.

How do I log past time?

Tap an activity, then select "Log Time" to manually enter hours and minutes for a past session.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your Apple Watch. Nothing is sent to the internet or shared with third parties.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual activities and goals within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Is the timer completely accurate?

Timer durations are approximate. Do not use for medical, billing, or other purposes requiring precise time tracking.
