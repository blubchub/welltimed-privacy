WellTimed Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

Overview

WellTimed is a time tracking app for Apple Watch. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

WellTimed does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data — including activities, time entries, goals, and streaks — is stored locally on your Apple Watch using on-device storage.

No Analytics or Tracking

WellTimed does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. We do not track your usage or behavior.

No Network Access

WellTimed does not connect to the internet. All app functionality works entirely offline on your device.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your Apple Watch using UserDefaults and App Groups. This data stays on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling WellTimed from your Apple Watch.

Children's Privacy

WellTimed does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
